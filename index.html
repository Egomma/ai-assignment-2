Who I Am

About Me

I am a practicing lawyer, lifelong learner, and technology enthusiast passionate about using innovation to solve real-world problems. My journey into technology began with a desire to expand my skills and create solutions that make meaningful impact on people's lives.

I have completed training in HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS and I am currently advancing my knowledge in JavaScript and Artificial Intelligence. I believe in continuous learning and personal growth, and I am committed to mastering every skill I set out to learn.

My vision is to bridge the gap between law and technology by developing digital solutions that improve access to legal services, empower communities, and create opportunities for collaboration and growth. I am particularly interested in building platforms that enable people to access legal assistance remotely, connect with like-minded individuals in the tech ecosystem, and unlock global opportunities.

As I continue my journey in technology, I remain driven by curiosity, innovation, and a desire to create tools that make a positive difference in society.
